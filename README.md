## 🌍 The Problem

In every city, thousands of gig workers operate daily --- yet they
remain **invisible in official statistics**.

This leads to:

-   ❌ Inaccurate workforce estimates\
-   ❌ Inefficient urban planning\
-   ❌ Weak economic decision-making

> **What is not measured... cannot be managed.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 💡 The Solution --- GigScope

**GigScope** is an AI-powered observatory that converts scattered
signals into **real-time workforce intelligence**.
